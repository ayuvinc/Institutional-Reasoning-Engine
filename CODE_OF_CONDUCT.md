# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment for all participants.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus critique on ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Help keep discussion actionable and evidence-based.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Abusive language, threats, or intimidation.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors for violations.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the maintainer listed in `README.md` Author section.
